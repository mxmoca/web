What even is chess?

      You probably have known that chess existed all your life, but maybe you just remembered of its existance from the recent release of the show Queens Gambit. Although they were slightly inaccurate in the pacing of Beth's rapid growth, overall, chess was represented pretty well; even the positions played were playable variations. Chess is one of the oldest games in the world dating back over 1500 years. The game of chess has evolved as it spread around the globe to the game we play today. As a result,this journey has brought people together from different cultures, ages, and backgrounds over a common bond and passion for the game.
