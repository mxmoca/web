Chess

The Pieces

  • Pawn
    • Worth 1 point
    • Moves 1 square at a time, forward only
    • Can move two squares forward if in starting square
  • Knight
    • Worth 3 points
    • Moves 2 square in one direction then 1 square right or left
    • Can jump over other pieces
  • Bishop
    • Worth 3 points
    • Moves along diagonals
  • Rock
    • Worth 5 points
    • Moves along files and rows
    • Participates in castle
  • Queen
    • Worth 9 points
    • Moves along files, rows, and diagonals
    • Most powerful piece
  • King
    • Worth the game
    • Moves one square in any direction
    • Participates in castle
